AlkaThinTech

Molecular precursors for next-generation lithium niobate thin films

About

AlkaThinTech develops patented molecular precursor technologies enabling scalable deposition of oriented lithium niobate thin films for integrated photonics and advanced electro-optic applications.

Technology

  • Patented aminoalkoxide-based lithium precursors
  • DLI-CVD thin film deposition
  • Controlled oligomerization (dimer to hexamer systems)
  • Oriented LiNbO₃ thin films (Z-cut compatible)

Applications

  • Integrated photonics
  • Space communications (ESA-related technologies)
  • RF photonics and modulators
  • Quantum and nonlinear optics
