Technology Platform

AlkaThinTech develops a proprietary molecular precursor platform enabling the controlled deposition of oriented lithium niobate (LiNbO₃) thin films via DLI-CVD processes for integrated photonics and RF applications.

  • Proprietary lithium precursor chemistry
  • Tunable oligomerization: dimeric to hexameric architectures
  • Direct growth of functional LiNbO₃ thin films
  • Crystallographic orientation control (Z-axis compatible)

Molecular Chemistry Platform

At the heart of AlkaThinTech lies a breakthrough in alkali metal precursor chemistry. We have designed and synthesized a new family of highly innovative molecules based on lithium (Li), sodium (Na) and potassium (K) — purpose-built for thin-film deposition processes.

CVD & ALD Compatible

These precursors are fully compatible with both Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) and Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD) processes, enabling precise thickness control from a few nanometers to several microns. They have already been successfully demonstrated in both deposition modes.

Engineered for Volatility & Stability

The molecular architecture is tuned to deliver optimal vapor pressure, thermal stability, and controlled reactivity at the substrate surface — key requirements for reproducible, high-purity thin-film growth in industrial settings.
