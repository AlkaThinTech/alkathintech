AlkaThinTech

Molecular precursor engineering for scalable lithium niobate thin-film photonics
PATENTED CHEMISTRY • THIN FILM DEPOSITION • INTEGRATED PHOTONICS

Technology Platform

AlkaThinTech develops a proprietary molecular precursor platform enabling the controlled deposition of oriented lithium niobate (LiNbO₃) thin films via DLI-CVD processes.

  • Patented aminoalkoxide-based lithium precursors
  • Tunable oligomerization: dimeric to hexameric architectures
  • Direct growth of functional LiNbO₃ thin films
  • Crystallographic orientation control (Z-axis compatible)

Strategic Applications

  • Integrated electro-optic photonic circuits
  • Space-qualified optical communication systems (ESA-relevant)
  • RF photonics and high-speed modulators
  • Quantum photonics and nonlinear optical systems

Core Value Proposition

A full-stack approach combining molecular design, precursor synthesis, and thin-film deposition enables atomic-level control of material formation, offering a scalable route to next-generation LiNbO₃ photonic platforms.
