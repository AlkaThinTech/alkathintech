Technology Platform

AlkaThinTech develops a proprietary molecular precursor platform enabling the controlled deposition of oriented lithium niobate (LiNbO₃) thin films via DLI-CVD processes for integrated photonics and RF applications.

  • Proprietary lithium precursor chemistry
  • Tunable oligomerization: dimeric to hexameric architectures
  • Direct growth of functional LiNbO₃ thin films
  • Crystallographic orientation control (Z-axis compatible)

DLI-CVD Process Schema

Direct Liquid Injection CVD process for lithium niobate thin-film deposition
